**Test:** Create a binary market with auto-resolution enabled (city: "Paris", condition: "rain", deadline: a near-future date). Allow the Celery beat task to fire at the deadline. Verify the market resolves automatically without proposer action.

**Why human:** Requires running Docker stack, Celery worker, real clock, and live external API call. The backend wiring is confirmed (schema field → json.dumps → Bet row → resolution task key contract matches), but the full round-trip cannot be tested statically.

#### 3. Payout Breakdown section on a resolved market

**Test:** Find or create a resolved/closed market with at least one winner. Navigate to that market's detail page. Verify the green "Payout Breakdown" section appears and shows correct data.
**Expected:** Green-bordered section with "Payout Breakdown" heading, table of winners with BP Won and TP Won columns. Empty or absent section for open markets.
**Why human:** Requires a market in "closed" status with BpTransaction(reason=bet_win) records. Static analysis confirms the conditional (`market?.status === "closed"`) and the query/render logic are correctly wired, but actual display requires a resolved market in the DB.
