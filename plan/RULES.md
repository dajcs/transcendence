Simplified voting.
Type of bets:
- Yes/No (binary)
- Multiple choice
- Numeric (predict a number within a range)

Every vote costs 1 bp (betting point)
The probabilities are constantly refreshed.
A bet can be withdrawn and it will be reimbursed with the winning probability of the position at the withdrawal time - e.g. if position has a 10% winning probability at withdrawal then 0.1 bp will be reimbursed.
A winning bet is rewarded with +1bp and +1tp

points:
- kp: karma points, get for **new** upvotes on comments or proposed bets (daily reset)
- bp: betting points, get +10 at subscription, +1 at daily login, +log(kp), +1 to vote for your own bet
- tp: truth points, +1 for a winning bet
- sp: spice points - the amount won at pairwise bets


pairwise bets: the betting amount is between 1 and 99 cents so the total is 1 EUR. Can not be withdrawn. The winner takes the 1 EUR.
